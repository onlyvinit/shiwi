Passionate about empowering communities, advocating for equity, and building sustainable systems that uplift lives. My Journey in Social Development

I began my academic path with a focus on the humanities, earning my higher secondary education in the arts stream. Driven by a passion for understanding societal structures and advocating for positive change, I pursued a Bachelor’s degree in Sociology. This academic foundation provided me with critical insights into social systems, inequality, and community dynamics.

Following graduation, I was selected as a "Gandhi Fellow" with the Dhruvo Tara, where I worked closely with grassroots communities, local governance systems, and educational stakeholders. My role emphasized leadership development, capacity building, and system-level transformation in underserved areas.

Prior to this fellowship, I contributed to social change initiatives through my work with the NGO "Piramal Foundation", serving as a dedicated team member and social worker. There, I engaged in community outreach programs, youth mobilization, and awareness campaigns focused on education, health, and equity.

These diverse experiences have equipped me with a deep understanding of the challenges communities face, as well as the tools to co-create sustainable, people-centered solutions. My journey continues to be fueled by a commitment to social justice, inclusion, and collaborative growth.

Project Studies

Dhruvo Tara: Community Engagement

As a Gandhi Fellow with the Dhruvo Tara, I spearheaded initiatives to enhance community engagement through extracurricular activities in rural schools. My work focused on fostering creativity and holistic development among students by organizing activities like drawing, storytelling, and interactive workshops. A key highlight was conducting sessions on women’s hygiene, empowering young girls with knowledge and resources to prioritize their health. These efforts not only boosted student participation but also strengthened community trust in educational systems, contributing to sustainable change in underserved areas.

Piramal Foundation: Gram Sabha Awareness

At Piramal Foundation, I led campaigns to raise awareness about Gram Panchayat Development Plans (GPDP) and Local Sustainable Development Goals (LSDG). Through community outreach across multiple villages, I facilitated discussions and workshops to encourage active participation in Gram Sabhas. My efforts empowered residents to voice their needs, influencing local governance and resource allocation. By fostering inclusive dialogue, these initiatives strengthened community ownership and accountability, laying the groundwork for sustainable development and equitable progress.

Piramal Foundation: Child Rights and Empowerment

My work with Piramal Foundation included impactful programs to educate children on their rights, waste management, mental health, and civic responsibilities. I organized interactive sessions, including a mock child election to teach the importance of voting, and encouraged volunteering to build leadership skills. Collaborations with institutions for mental health counseling provided critical support to young participants. These initiatives empowered children to advocate for themselves and their communities, fostering a sense of agency and responsibility.

Piramal Foundation: Community and School Collaborations

I facilitated meetings with School Management Committees (SMCs), parents, and teachers to address waste management and promote extracurricular activities. These collaborations enhanced school environments by integrating community-driven solutions, such as waste segregation programs. Additionally, I organized a cleanliness campaign in Mehla, mobilizing residents to clean public spaces, fostering civic pride and environmental awareness. My interactions with local authorities, including the Block Development Officer (BDO), further amplified the impact of these initiatives, strengthening community ties.

My Goals

Short-Term

In the next two years, I aim to develop and scale community-driven programs that enhance access to quality education and healthcare in underserved regions. By partnering with local NGOs and schools, I will create tailored workshops and resources that empower youth and women, fostering skills for self-reliance and leadership. My focus will be on measurable outcomes, such as increased school attendance and health awareness, to build a foundation for sustainable community growth.

Mid-Term

Over the next five years, I plan to establish a regional network of social development practitioners to share best practices and innovate solutions for systemic challenges. By leveraging data-driven insights and grassroots feedback, I aim to design inclusive models for local governance and resource allocation. My goal is to empower at least 50 communities to actively participate in decision-making processes, ensuring equitable access to public services and opportunities.

Long-Term

My long-term vision is to influence national policy frameworks to prioritize social equity and sustainable development. By advocating for marginalized voices and collaborating with policymakers, I aspire to shape legislation that addresses systemic inequalities in education, healthcare, and economic opportunities. My ultimate goal is to contribute to a future where every individual, regardless of background, has the tools and opportunities to thrive in an inclusive society.
